# Sanctuary‑Development

Welcome to the official GitHub organisation for **Sanctuary‑Development**.

## About Us
Sanctuary‑Development is the home of software engineering and technical innovation for Sanctuary Group in the UK.  
We use this space to organise, collaborate on, and manage development work across a range of internal technology initiatives.

## What We Do
- Maintain and develop software solutions that support Sanctuary’s operational and digital needs.
- Collaborate on shared libraries, tools and internal development standards.
- Explore modern development practices, automation and cloud technologies to continuously improve our engineering ecosystem.

## Security & Privacy
This organisation hosts a combination of private repositories and controlled-access projects.  
We do not publish internal or sensitive information publicly.  
Access to repositories is granted based on project roles and organisational policy.

## Contributing
At this time, contributions are limited to approved Sanctuary team members and partners.  
If you have been invited to join, please ensure:
- You follow our internal development guidelines  
- You maintain secure handling of all code and assets  
- You adhere to Sanctuary’s engineering standards

## Contact
For enquiries related to this organisation, access, or collaboration, please contact the Sanctuary technology team through internal channels.
